Power 80 Accuracy 100 PP 10
Priority 0 Additional effect chance 0%
Additional effect description User must wait a turn before use, but is underwater and invulnerable to most attacks during that turn.

Dives on the first turn, floats up and attacks on the next turn. Can be used to dive deep into water.
